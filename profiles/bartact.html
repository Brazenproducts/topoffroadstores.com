Bartact

Made-in-USA tactical seat covers and accessories — the manufacturer that sets the standard

FULL PROFILE

Bartact isn't just a seat cover company — they're the company that other seat cover companies measure themselves against. Based in Temecula, California, Bartact designs and manufactures everything in-house using materials that would satisfy a military spec sheet: UV-protected polyester, 1000-denier Cordura nylon, foam-and-scrim backing, and double or triple stitching on every seam. Every cover is SRS airbag compatible. They're Berry Amendment compliant — meaning their products meet U.S. military procurement standards for domestic manufacturing. Their Jeep seat cover line is the gold standard, but they also make paracord grab handles (they invented the category — everyone else is copying them), limit straps (the world's largest manufacturer), MOLLE panels, sun visors, and a growing range of accessories. When we say Bartact is #1, we're not being diplomatic — their products are objectively the best-built in their categories.
