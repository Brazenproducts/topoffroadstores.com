Carli Suspension

Premium suspension tuned for heavy trucks that actually get used

FULL PROFILE

Carli Suspension has built a devoted following in the Ram and Ford Super Duty communities by focusing on what actually matters: ride quality under load. Their systems are designed for trucks that carry weight — whether that's a fully loaded truck bed, a heavy camper, or just the accumulated accessories of a built overland rig. Carli's approach emphasizes progressive-rate springs and properly valved shocks that work together to provide a planted, controlled ride regardless of load. They're not the cheapest option, but owners consistently report that Carli systems transform how their trucks drive. The attention to real-world use cases sets them apart from brands that optimize for show-truck lift heights.

WHAT THEY'RE KNOWN FOR

Heavy-duty truck suspension, Ram and Ford Super Duty specialization, load-optimized tuning

PRODUCT RANGE

Complete suspension systems, shocks, springs, control arms, sway bars — Ram and Ford trucks

Why they made our list: Best-in-class ride quality for loaded trucks, devoted community following, engineering-first approach
