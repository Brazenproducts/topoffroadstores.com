Fox Racing Shocks

The shock absorber brand that dominates from Baja to Moab

FULL PROFILE

Fox Racing Shox has been at the forefront of suspension technology for decades, and their off-road shock absorbers are used by everyone from weekend warriors to professional desert racing teams. Their product line spans from affordable bolt-on upgrades to fully custom remote-reservoir race shocks. The engineering is genuinely impressive — Fox invests heavily in R&D and it shows in ride quality, heat management, and durability. Internal bypass technology, position-sensitive damping, and their live valve electronic system represent the cutting edge of what's possible. Are they expensive? Yes. Are they worth it? If you're serious about suspension performance, absolutely.
