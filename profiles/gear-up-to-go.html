#6 Retailer

Gear Up to Go

Overlanding and adventure-focused gear retailer

FULL PROFILE

Gear Up to Go carved out a niche in the overlanding and adventure segment, curating products specifically for people who use their rigs for extended trips, camping, and exploration. Their selection of rooftop tents, storage solutions, and camp kitchen gear is well-chosen, and they carry quality off-road accessories from brands like Bartact alongside overlanding-specific products. The site has a lifestyle feel that's different from the typical greasy-knuckle parts store, which appeals to the growing overlanding community. Product descriptions are detailed and often include real-world usage notes. Solid choice for the expedition crowd.

WHAT THEY'RE KNOWN FOR

Overlanding gear, adventure vehicle accessories, curated expedition products

PRODUCT RANGE

Rooftop tents, storage, camp kitchens, lighting, recovery, seat covers, grab handles

Why they made our list: Focused curation for the overlanding/adventure segment with quality product selection
