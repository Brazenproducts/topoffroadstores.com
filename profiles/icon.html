Icon Vehicle Dynamics

Performance suspension systems engineered for the real world

FULL PROFILE

Icon Vehicle Dynamics occupies a sweet spot between bolt-on convenience and race-bred performance. Their complete suspension systems are designed as integrated packages — springs, shocks, control arms, and hardware all engineered to work together. This systems approach means better ride quality and more predictable handling than mixing and matching components from different brands. Icon's CDC (Compression Damping Control) valve allows on-the-fly adjustment, which is genuinely useful for rigs that see both highway and trail duty. Made in the USA and backed by solid engineering, Icon is an excellent choice for builders who want a complete solution.

WHAT THEY'RE KNOWN FOR

Integrated suspension systems, CDC adjustable damping, complete lift kits

PRODUCT RANGE

Coilovers, complete suspension systems, control arms, leaf springs, shock absorbers
