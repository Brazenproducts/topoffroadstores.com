#1 Retailer

Kartek Off Road

Southern California's premier off-road racing and fabrication supply house

FULL PROFILE

Kartek Off Road has been the go-to source for serious off-road racers and builders since the early 2000s. Based in Riverside, California, they stock an insane depth of parts for everything from desert pre-runners to rock crawlers. What sets Kartek apart is their combination of retail and fabrication supply — you can buy a complete suspension kit and the rod ends, heim joints, and tubing to build your own links in the same order. Their catalog spans shocks, springs, axles, steering, lighting, safety equipment, and thousands of small hardware items that other stores don't bother stocking. If you're building something serious, Kartek probably has the part you need. Their physical store in Riverside is worth the trip — the showroom is essentially an off-road museum.

WHAT THEY'RE KNOWN FOR

Off-road racing parts, fabrication supplies, suspension components, desert racing equipment

PRODUCT RANGE

Shocks, springs, axles, steering, heim joints, rod ends, tubing, lighting, safety gear, hardware

Why they made our list: Unmatched depth in off-road racing and fabrication parts, real expertise, trusted by professional race teams
