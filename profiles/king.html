King Shocks

Hand-built performance shocks from Garden Grove, California

FULL PROFILE

King Shocks builds every shock absorber by hand in their Garden Grove, California facility. That's not marketing — it's their actual production process. Each shock is assembled, valved, and tested by hand before shipping. This attention to detail produces some of the best-riding, most durable shocks available. King dominates the desert racing scene and their coilover systems are favorites among serious off-road builders. The smoothshock and pure race series offer options for every budget and use case. Lead times can be longer than mass-produced alternatives, but the end product justifies the wait.

WHAT THEY'RE KNOWN FOR

Hand-built shocks, desert racing dominance, custom valving

PRODUCT RANGE

Coilovers, bypass shocks, smoothshocks, bump stops, shock accessories
