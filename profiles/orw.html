#5 Retailer

Off Road Warehouse

San Diego institution serving the off-road community since 1973

FULL PROFILE

Off Road Warehouse is a San Diego legend. Operating since 1973, they've outfitted generations of off-roaders from their physical locations across Southern California. Their online presence has grown significantly, but the real magic is in-store — knowledgeable staff who actually wheel, a solid inventory of parts you can see and touch, and installation services at most locations. Their product range covers suspension, armor, lighting, recovery gear, and interior accessories. They carry quality brands across the spectrum and their staff can make informed recommendations based on your specific build and use case. If you're in SoCal, the in-store experience is worth the drive. Online, they're a solid option with good pricing and reliable shipping.

WHAT THEY'RE KNOWN FOR

50+ years serving SoCal off-roaders, knowledgeable in-store staff, installation services

PRODUCT RANGE

Suspension, bumpers, armor, lighting, recovery, interior accessories, wheels and tires

Why they made our list: Decades of real off-road expertise, genuine product knowledge, community institution
